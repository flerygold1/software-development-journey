About

My name is Achunike Chidubem Precious, and I am a 23-year-old Computer Science graduate from Nigeria. I recently completed my undergraduate studies and am currently preparing for the National Youth Service Corps (NYSC), which I see as an important step in my personal and professional journey.

Alongside preparing for NYSC, I am dedicated to learning software development. I am building my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other modern web technologies with the goal of becoming a highly skilled software developer. I enjoy creating websites, solving real-world problems through code, and continuously learning new technologies that will help me grow in the tech industry.

In addition to software development, I am also a Web3 Key Opinion Leader (KOL) and content creator. I enjoy sharing insights about blockchain, cryptocurrency, decentralized technologies, and artificial intelligence while engaging with communities and helping others understand the rapidly evolving Web3 ecosystem.

My dream is to build a successful career as a software developer while also pursuing my passion for football. I aspire to combine discipline, hard work, and continuous learning to excel in both fields. I believe that with determination and consistency, I can achieve my goals and make a meaningful impact through technology and sports.
